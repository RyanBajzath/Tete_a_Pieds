Our Team

Cristina Yu

Cristina Yu is a highly skilled and experienced Registered Massage Therapist (LMT) and Manual Lymphatic Drainage Specialist (MLD-C). With an impressive blend of Eastern and Western medical knowledge, Cristina brings a unique perspective to her practice, offering her clients a holistic approach to wellness and healing. She is a proud member of the Fédération Québécoise des Massothérapeutes agréés (FQM) in Quebec, demonstrating her commitment to maintaining the highest standards of professional practice and continuing education in massage therapy with over 10 years of experience.

Prior to her career in massage therapy, Cristina worked as an internal medicine physician in China for over 20 years. This clinical experience gives her a deep understanding of human anatomy and physiology, as well as insight into various medical conditions and their effects on the body. Cristina's unique blend of massage therapy expertise and medical background allows her to offer a holistic approach to her clients' well-being. She understands the intricate connections between the body's various systems and how manual therapies can positively impact overall health.
