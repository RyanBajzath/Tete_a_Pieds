Aimee (Chunyan) Yu

Aimee (Chunyan) Yu is a member of the Fédération Québécoise des Massothérapeutes Agréés (FQM). A former internal medicine physician in China, with over 20 years of experience, Aimee received a Diploma in Advanced Clinical Massage Therapy (3000-hour program) from Makami College, Edmonton, Alberta, in 2016. Aimee is highly skilled and offers a multitude of massage techniques and expertise, including Swedish massage, TuiNa techniques, Scalp and Face Meridians and Acu-points work, Prenatal and Postnatal Massage, Cupping therapy, Hot stones massage, Manual Lymph Drainage (MLD), and Reflexology. Before dedicating herself to massage therapy, Aimee worked as a staff doctor in internal medicine, cardiologist, and endocrinologist. Aimee brings a wealth of knowledge to her practice. Aimee is an excellent communicator and empathetic listener. She always upholds the highest standards of discretion and professionalism.

Jinny (JingRu )Jian

Jinny is an experienced professional massage therapist with more than 6 years in the field and a proud member of the Réseau des Massothérapeutes Professionnels du Québec (RMPQ).

Her expertise includes:

  • Swedish massage
  • Hot stone therapy
  • Reflexology
  • Thai table massage

In addition to these specialties, Jiny is proficient in cupping, gua sha, and myofascial release therapy, providing effective methods to relieve muscle tension, reduce pain, and improve flexibility and overall well-being.

Committed to delivering professional, comfortable, and personalized care, Jinny aims to help each client find balance and relaxation. She invites you to experience her outstanding massage therapy services.
