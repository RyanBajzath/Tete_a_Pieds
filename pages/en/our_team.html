Aimee (Chunyan) Yu

Professional Massage Therapist | Former Internal Medicine Physician

Aimée (Chunyan) Yu is a member of the Fédération Québécoise des Massothérapeutes Agréés (FQM)

A former internal medicine physician, in China, with over 20 years of experience. Aimee received a Diploma in Advanced Clinical Massage Therapy (3000-hours program) from Makami College,Edmonton, Alberta in 2016.  

Aimee is highly skilled and offers a multitude of massage techniques and expertise, including: 

  • Manual Lymph Drainage (MLD) 
  • Reflexology
  • TuiNa techniques
  • Swedish massage 
  • Cupping therapy
  • Scalp and Face Meridians and Acu-points work
  • Prenatal and Postnatal Massage
  • Hot stones massage
  • TMJD (Temporomandibular joint disorder)

Before dedicating herself to massage therapy, Aimée worked as a staff doctor in internal medicine, Cardiologist doctor and Endocrinologist doctor. Aimee brings a wealth of knowledge to her practice. Aimee is an excellent communicator and empathetic listener. She always upholds the highest standards of discretion and professionalism.

Jinny (JingRu )Jian

Professional Massage Therapist

Jinny is an experienced professional massage therapist with more than 6 years in the field and a proud member of the Réseau des Massothérapeutes Professionnels du Québec (RMPQ).

Her expertise includes:

  • Swedish massage
  • Hot stone therapy
  • Reflexology
  • Thai table massage

In addition to these specialties, Jiny is proficient in cupping, gua sha, and myofascial release therapy, providing effective methods to relieve muscle tension, reduce pain, and improve flexibility and overall well-being.

Committed to delivering professional, comfortable, and personalized care, Jinny aims to help each client find balance and relaxation. She invites you to experience her outstanding massage therapy services.
